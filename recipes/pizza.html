HAWAIIAN BBQ VEGAN PIZZA

Pizza pizza :D

This pizza could be a horror for the purists, but the truth is that it is delicious

Ingredients :

  • 1/2 block extra-firm tofu (8 ounces)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
  • 1 Recipe Easy Stretchy Vegan Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 pizza dough, store bought or homemade
  • 1/3 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh pineapple, chopped
  • cilantro, optional for topping

Instructions :

  1. If you are making your own barbecue sauce and pizza dough, and vegan mozzarella, grab the recipes and make those first.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Pat tofu dry with paper towels, and cut into very small pieces (1/4 inch). Toss in a bowl with the soy sauce, place on parchment paper lined baking sheet, and bake for 25 minutes.
  4. Remove from the oven, mix in 1-2 tablespoons of barbecue sauce, and put back in the oven for 10 more minutes. Set aside. Increase the oven to 425 degrees F.
  5. Roll out your pizza dough, place it on a pizza pan, and spread with about 1/3 cup of the barbecue sauce. Top with the tofu pieces, dollops of cheese, red onion slices and pineapple.
  6. Bake for about 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden brown. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro and red pepper flakes, if desired. Slice and serve.
